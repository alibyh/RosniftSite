**If the cargo is less than 10 tons, the weight is considered to be the minimum possible in the tariff – 10 tons.*

Formula for calculating the cost (S)
S = weight (tons) * distance (km) * rate

Examples:

Need to deliver 6.794 tons of cargo over a distance of 350 km. Since the cargo is less than 10 tons, the weight in the formula will be 10 tons.
S = 10.000 tons * 350 km * 13.73 RUB = 48,055.00 RUB

Need to deliver 25.467 tons of cargo over a distance of 3,050 km.
S = 25.467 tons * 3,050 km * 8.89 RUB = 690,524.97 RUB
